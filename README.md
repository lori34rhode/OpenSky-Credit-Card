# OpenSky Credit Card Expense Tracker

## Description
The OpenSky Credit Card Expense Tracker is a dedicated financial management application designed specifically for individuals utilizing secure credit solutions to establish, monitor, and optimize their credit profiles. Managing a secured card effectively requires vigilant tracking of your available credit line, statement balances, and utilization ratios. This intuitive application empowers cardholders to log transactions in real-time, categorize statements, and visualize financial behaviors directly impacting their credit scores. 

By prioritizing crisp data design and instant mathematical calculations, this tool removes the guesswork from credit management. Users can track standard purchases, monitor recurring monthly subscription charges, and view generated digital receipts to reconcile their official OpenSky statements seamlessly. Maintaining your credit utilization below the recommended 30% threshold is made effortless through structural monitoring components built straight into the user interface. Whether you are actively rebuilding your credit history or establishing financial independence for the first time, this repository provides the local framework necessary to manage your OpenSky Credit Card liabilities confidently, accurately, and securely.

## Features
*   **Real-Time Statement Calculation:** Instant updates to your current balance and remaining credit line.
*   **Smart Categorization:** Tag expenses dynamically to track exactly where your funds are allocated.
*   **Utilization Safeguards:** Clear UI indications to assist in keeping monthly utilization within optimal ranges.
*   **Receipt Generator:** Creates structured digital summaries for seamless monthly statement reconciliation.

## Technologies Used
*   **React.js (v18+)** - Core frontend user interface architecture
*   **CSS3** - Customized responsive styling layout
*   **JavaScript (ES6+)** - Dynamic state manipulation and transaction logic
